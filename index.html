Instructions if you want to use your keyboard

  • Press Spacebar to shoot the gun
  • Move the ship with the left and right arrows
  • Press "r" to reset

Instructions if you want to use your mouse

  • Use your mouse to move the spaceship
  • Click on the screen to shoot gun
  • Click on the Reset button to reset

If you lose controls, click inside the game and try again.
